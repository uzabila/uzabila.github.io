Tennis in Croatia


Tennis Academy coordinates: GPS N45°27.41 E13°30.52

We represent the Croatian Tennis Academy, which is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, 2 km from the Croatian city of Umag. The city is known for hosting the annual ATP 250 tournament.

The nearest airports: Pula (Croatia) - 90 km, Trieste (Italy) - 90 km, Rijeka (Croatia) - 125 km, Ljubljana (Slovenia) - 150 km, Venice (Italy) - 205 km, Zagreb (Croatia) - 280 km.

The nearest railway stations are in Koper and Trieste.

For questions about organizing training and summer holidays in Croatia, you can contact us using any of the contacts below.
